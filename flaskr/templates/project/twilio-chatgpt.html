Introduction

As it stands now, people can only access/interact with the ChatGPT over their website, not very chat-ish in this era of smartphones. I want to make it available on mobile through SMS so that people can combine the power of language models with modern day smartphones' powerful voice-to-text capabilities. Basically allowing people to "talk" to ChatGPT.

How to use this app?

I am glad you asked! You can text to +18554592159 from your mobile phone (normal text rate may apply) and start chatting with ChatGPT.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT in a nutshell is the most powerful natural language model that's publicly available. Simply put, it's future happening in front of your eyes.

Twilio

Twilio provides amazing APIs that allow interactions between web Apps and SMS. Using twilio's services, I could

  • Obtain a phone number
  • receive text messages from the phone number
  • port text received over the cloud server to ChatGPT's API
  • send back ChatGPT's response to the original sender
